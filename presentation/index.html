❄ Onboarding
Joining the club

Invite-only. Safety first.

An invite code from the club gets you in. A versioned safety briefing keeps everyone honest about what cold water can do — when the club updates it, members re-confirm.

  • Invite code rotates per season — admins control it
  • Safety terms versioned and acknowledged
  • Liability waiver captured at signup
❄ Feed
The Swims tab

The home screen.

Yesterday's stats up top, your weekly goal underneath, upcoming events with avatars of who's going, then a feed of everyone's swims with photos, conditions, fist bumps, and comments.

  • Real-time — others' swims appear as they log
  • Swim summary: distance, temperature, duration, weather labels
  • Tap a swim for the full detail view
❄ Feed
Swim detail

Every swim has a story.

Photo, distance, temperature, time in water, conditions, who you swam with, notes. Comments below for the conversation. Fist bumps for the quick reactions.

  • Multi-photo carousel
  • @-mentions in notes and comments
  • Live-updating comments and reactions
❄ Feed
Logging

One sheet. Done in 20 seconds.

Tap the + from anywhere, fill the obvious fields, tag your weather and buddies, hit Save. Photo optional. Privacy lock optional.

  • Smart defaults: location, time, weather rolled forward
  • Buddy autocomplete from club roster
  • Counts toward club achievements when logged at Sandkaj
❄ Stats
The Stats tab

Numbers that mean something.

Trends across the season — swims, distance, duration — with average water temperature overlaid. Correlations: temperature vs. distance, vs. time, distance vs. time. Not a generic dashboard.

  • Filter by rolling window or by month
  • Past seasons reachable for the long view
  • Personal activity below the club view
❄ Events
The Events tab

Self-organize.

Next Up at the top — what's happening soonest, who's going. Calendar strip for the week. RSVP in one tap. Recurring weekly events for the AGM, lunch swims, anything regular.

  • Anyone can create an event
  • Weekly recurrences capped to season end (Apr 30)
  • Going / Cancel RSVP, optimistic updates

Recent activity, all-time stats, your swim history. Your settings, including whether you're hidden from the leaderboard. Your color, your name, your story.

❄ Privacy
Privacy & control

The data is the members'. Always.

Five concrete commitments — built into the app, not just the policy page.

1

Members-only by design

Only approved club members can join, and only members see club swims. No public feed, no search engine indexing, no “guest” tier.

2

Public or private — your call, per swim

Toggle any swim private at log time or after. The board can still see aggregates for engagement tracking — never the identifying details of a private swim.

3

Opt out of leaderboards & charts

One toggle in Settings hides you from the top-5 leaderboard and anonymizes your dot in correlation charts. Your swims still appear in your own stats; nobody else can identify them.

4

Delete swims or your whole account

Swim-by-swim or all-at-once. Your data is removed from storage within 5 minutes — no soft-delete, no grace-period inbox.

5

Nothing leaves NIS

No analytics shared with third parties, no ads. The wellbeing-research integration we're scoping is fully opt-in and anonymous — you choose, swim by swim.

❄ Admin
Engagement & safety upkeep

Tools the board runs on.

Two ongoing jobs in-app: review member engagement against the season's criteria — has each member met the buddy and cold-swim minimums? — and keep the safety briefing current as the club evolves what it expects of swimmers.

  • Per-season scorecard: buddy criterion, cold-swim criterion, events hosted
  • Filter by criterion status, drill into individual members
  • Edit and version the safety briefing — members re-acknowledge on next launch
❄ Admin
Onboarding the next member

How new joiners get in.

Two-step gate: rotate the invite code each season so it doesn't leak indefinitely, then review and approve every signup before they can post or RSVP.

  • One-tap invite-code rotation, scoped per season
  • Pending queue surfaces every signup awaiting board approval
  • Approve or reject — admins decide who's actually in the club
