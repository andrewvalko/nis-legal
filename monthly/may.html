What shipped

35 headline features shipped — the highlights.

⭐

Catch up on what changed v1.7.5

Open the app after an update and you'll get a sheet listing what shipped in the versions you missed — no more guessing, and we'll never re-show the same cards.

⭐

Announcement polish pass v1.7.4

The pinned announcement card on your inbox has a new green header. Tapping a reactor jumps to their profile, the dismiss X actually sticks across app launches, and admins can edit or delete an announcement from a new "More" menu on the detail view.

⭐

Smoother onboarding for new members v1.7.3

The welcome flow now includes a friendly profile-photo step as its final page, and we hold off on asking for notification permission until you've actually finished the welcome — instead of the moment your account is created.

⭐

Delete your account v1.7.3

You can now permanently remove your account from Profile → Delete account. Your swims stay in club totals (anonymized) so the leaderboard isn't disrupted, but your name and login are gone.

⭐

Refreshed swim cards v1.7.6

A unified look for swim posts in the feed. Bigger distance and temperature, achievement chips tucked into the corner, write-in buddies as initial avatars, and the same hero shape whether your swim has a photo or not.
