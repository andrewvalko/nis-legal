❄ May 2026
What shipped

Apps are now live on the app stores!

The apps launched

NIS Tracker is now live on both the App Store and Google Play. May was spent getting the foundations right — polished onboarding, password reset, dark app icon on iOS, Inter font on Android, and a "What's new" sheet that greets members after each update. Signing up is simpler too: address, date of birth, and phone are no longer required upfront.

Community & social

The Sandkaj water-quality flag launched — members can report conditions in real time, visible right in the toolbar. Group swim cards now collapse simultaneous swims into one feed item with a face pile, and inline comments on events let you follow along without tapping in. A long-press lets you report or block another member's comment.

Events, rebuilt

Events got a full overhaul: a new form with types (Swim, Social, Dip & Sauna, Competition), recurring-event support, a green RSVP button, and a notify-the-club toggle when you post. Hosts get a push before their event starts with the current RSVP list, and admins can edit any event regardless of who created it.

Stats & analytics

The Correlations chart grew a personal trend line and a Me/Club toggle so you can compare your swims against the club. The Trends flyout now shows average water temperature alongside swim counts. In v1.9.5, Stats moved into the Overview tab, trimming the profile down to three clean tabs.
