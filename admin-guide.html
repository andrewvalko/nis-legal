A quick tour of the admin powers built into the NIS Tracker app. As an NIS board member, you're an admin by default.

Admin guide

You can moderate content and manage membership, plus a few extras. Everything here is built into the app — no separate dashboard, no web login.

From anywhere in the app

Delete things

Long-press any comment, swim, or event to delete it. Useful if something's offensive or inappropriate.

Approve new members

You'll get a notification when someone signs up or is approved. There may already be a few pending right now that need one of us to approve.

Remove or lapse members

Mark individuals as banned, lapsed, or delete them outright. Most of this lives on individual profiles or the "club view" at the bottom of the Club page.

Admin control panel

You may have noticed a shield icon next to the notification bell in the upper right — that's how you get in.

Pending Approvals

Sign-ups waiting for review. The app is in the public App Store, so we need to be careful to only approve people we actually know are in or joining the club.

Reports

If a user reports content as inappropriate, it shows up here. Apple made me add this; hopefully we never use it.

Safety Terms

What users agree to at sign-up — release of liability and so on. I copied it from the document I signed when I joined; edit as you see fit.

Heads up  Every edit forces the whole club to re-consent.

Invite Code

Bypasses the pending step at sign-up. Pass it to new members during onboarding. Admins can rotate it if it leaks.

Known Issue Banner

Mostly for me. Puts an alert at the top of a few pages when there's a bug affecting the app.

Board Tracking

Tracks requirements and nice-to-haves — swims with the club, swims under 5°, events created. Won't be useful out of season, but handy in October/November to see who met the bar. Have a look and let me know if anything's unclear.

New Announcement

Push an announcement to the whole club. Sends a push notification and pins it at the top of the inbox. Could replace email blasts.
