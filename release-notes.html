Release history

Each version lists what shipped in that Android release. iOS tracks the same cadence.

v1.8.9 May 16, 2026

Insight of the week

A new card at the top of the Club tab surfaces one curated factoid each week — which conditions make for longer swims, which days are busiest, how the past month compares to the one before. Refreshes Monday mornings.

Inline comments on events and grouped swims

Event cards and grouped swim cards now show the latest comments inline so you can read along without tapping in.

Trends flyout follows the chart

Tap a bar in the Club trends chart and the weekly summary anchors to the chart itself, not pinned to the top of the screen.

Heads-up banners

If something in the app is broken, admins can post a short heads-up at the top of Swims, Club, or Events. Tap × to dismiss; new alerts replace dismissed ones.

What's new, half-sheet

Release notes now open at half height with a drag handle, so you can still see the feed behind. Drag up for the full list.

Polish

Android pull-to-refresh spinner stays up through the full fetch. Today's events circle stays empty when no events remain. Faster splash and tab loads. Goal CTA reads "Set a weekly goal". Header-to-photo seam restored on grouped swim cards. Bathing-water flag peek aligns with the rest of the above-feed reveal.

v1.8.8 May 14, 2026

Event form, rebuilt

Create and edit screens are now a settings-style list — Basics, When & Where, Description, Options. Type and Location are full-width segmented; required fields show a small blue dot until they're filled.

Events tab refresh

Next Up hero at the top, this-week upcoming cards below, then a small "Further out" group for anything more than seven days away. Tighter rhythm and a clearer organizer pill in the corner.

Green Going button

RSVP'd? The button turns green and reads "Going". Tap again to un-RSVP. No separate corner checkmark.

Edit Event, fixed

Editing an event used to instantly close the screen on Android — that's gone. The edit form now also correctly hides Notify and Repeats (creation-only).

Polish

Long event names no longer clip the form label. Title field on iOS doesn't lose focus mid-typing anymore. Mini-feed row spacing matches between platforms. "Dip & Sauna" → "Sauna". "Competition" → "Race".

v1.8.7 May 13, 2026

Wetsuit label

Tag a swim as 'wetsuit' to mark it unofficial. Wetsuit swims only group with other wetsuit swims, same as pool swims.

Event forms, redrawn

Date and time are now full-width buttons. Time and location share a row. Saving requires an explicit time pick — no more accidental midnight events.

Admins can edit any event

Edit menu opens for admins on events they didn't create.

Day-of-week on event dates

Date row now reads 'Wed, May 13, 2026' so you know what day you're picking.

Tap-row toggles

Tapping anywhere on the Notify the club or Repeats weekly rows toggles them.

Polish

Time picker forces 12-hour AM/PM on iOS. Type is a dropdown on Android. Save lights up immediately after picking a time. Past-time warning on the event form. Server respects labels when deciding official status. Repeats-weekly checkbox no longer crashes.

v1.8.6 May 13, 2026

Admin Reject deletes the account

Rejecting a pending member from the admin panel now fully deletes the account and frees their email for a future re-application — used to soft-disable and leave a stub. Adds a confirmation step before the delete fires.

v1.8.5 May 13, 2026

Club / Me filter on the Scatterplot

New toggle on the Scatterplot — 'Me' hides the club's dots and dashed trend line so you see just your swims against your own solid trend line. Card header on the Stats tab; bottom-right of the expanded view.

Event time picker fixed on iOS

The time pill on the event form was unresponsive in 1.8.4 — tapping it didn't open anything. Replaced with a SwiftUI picker that snaps to 5-min increments on close. Tapping the time works again.

More Danish across the app

Filled ~80 missing translations — including the three new swim labels (cloudy/snowy/algae) which displayed as English in 1.8.4 on Danish phones. Also covers achievements, signup, wellbeing survey, water flag, and common UI buttons.

Polish

Names auto-trim leading/trailing spaces on signup. Scatterplot mode and scope picks now survive an Android rotation. Scatterplot card layout: chart options moved below the chart inside the same container; Expand is now a chip in that row.

v1.8.4 May 12, 2026

Three new swim labels

Cloudy, snowy, and algae join the conditions picker on the swim log. Tag a swim with what made it memorable.

The Scatterplot now has a second, solid trend line fit through just your swims — sits alongside the dashed club-wide line. On the expanded chart, your line recomputes as you pan and zoom, so it always reflects what's on screen. (The chart is now called Scatterplot instead of Correlations.)

Heads-up before your event starts

5-minute event times

Event start times snap to 5-minute increments now — useful for the 6:55am crowd. Swim log stays on 15-min steps.

Polish

Scatterplot card stays visible from 3 swims (was 5). Description field on event form lifts above the keyboard.

v1.8.3 May 11, 2026

Faster everywhere

Feed, Events, and Inbox now paint instantly from cache on cold launch — no more skeleton flash before content loads. Tapping a swim or event opens the detail view pre-populated from the list. New comments, reactions, and counts slide in instead of snapping.

Profile photos cached forever

Once a member's avatar is loaded, it stays cached — no more re-fetching on every screen. When someone uploads a new photo, the URL changes and the cache picks it up automatically.

Correlations chart has a trend line

Dashed regression line on the temp × distance / temp × time / distance × time scatter, so the chart actually shows the relationship instead of just a dot cloud. Axes also tightened — no more third of the chart wasted on whitespace.

Trends flyout shows avg water temp

Tap a day, week, or month on the Trends chart and you now also see the average water temp for that bucket, alongside swims, swimmers, distance, and duration.

Polish

Cleaner hero temp on swim cards — degree symbol scaled to match the number, no shrunken "C". Temperature still sits slightly subordinate to distance so distance stays the headline.

Recurring events: pick how many

Less facepile overload

The fist-bump face pile under each swim card is now just a number. Cuts down on the cluttered avatar walls and leaves more room for the rest of the row.

Polish
