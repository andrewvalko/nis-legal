What's New

Mid-April 2026

@Mentions

Type @ in a comment or swim notes to tag a club member. They get notified and their name shows up bold inline.

Edit your swims

Tap the menu on any of your swims to edit. Notes, buddies, and photos can change any time. Stats lock after 24 hours to protect leaderboards.

Live comments New!

Comments update in real time. If someone replies while you're looking at a swim or event, it just appears. Comment counts on the feed update live too.

Smarter notifications

Get pinged when someone joins a comment thread you're in. Mentions take priority so you won't get doubled up. New toggles in Settings let you tune it.

New swim fields

Warm-up/sauna time and real feel temperature. Real feel appears when you toggle air temp on. Both show on the swim detail view.

Club achievements

When a swim earns a club achievement, the badge shows as a chip in the Club summary card header. Tap it to jump to the swim.

Polish Updated!

Mentions show bold in feed comments. Bigger tap targets on the swim log form. Events show host and attendee count. Fist-bump lists use compact chips. Club totals only count official Sandkaj swims.

Early April 2026

Better feed cards

Swim notes show up top now. Duration on the right. Buddies truncate to two names + count. Social buttons are plain icons, not chunky chips.

Events in the feed

Comments inline below event cards. RSVP and comment count in their own row. Mini-event previews are tighter — day, time, title.

Air temp & water time

Two new optional fields. Air temp behind a snowflake toggle, water time behind a clock. Water time counts for charts and leaderboards.

Private swims

Lock icon on the swim log marks a swim as private. Still counts for your stats and achievements, just hidden from the club feed.

Redesigned forms

Swim log and event creation got a visual overhaul — cleaner card-based layout, more logical grouping. Same fields, easier to use.

Inline comments

Latest comments show directly on swim cards in the feed — no need to tap into the detail view to see the conversation.

Announcements

Bold and italic support. 2000-char limit with live counter. Long ones truncate with "show more". Write-in swim buddies for non-members.

Profile

Consistent white background across all tabs. Reshuffled to Achievements, Stats, Streak, Activity. About and Legal moved to Help & FAQ.

Polish & bug fixes

System fonts on both platforms. Consistent spacing everywhere. Card borders gone on Android. No more trailing dots on display names. Full Danish translation pass. Avatar updates reflect everywhere. Keyboard no longer covers text fields.

March 2026

Dark mode

Follows your phone's system setting. Every screen, chart, and card — all adapted.

Swim photos

Attach a photo when logging a swim. Shows as a hero image on your feed card. Tap "Add photo", pick an image, done.

Profile photos

Tap your avatar to upload a real photo. Replaces the colored circle everywhere — feed, comments, leaderboards.

Achievements

Earned badges show on your profile in a grid. Others can see them too. Progress bar for the next unlock.

New stats tab

Four charts: trends, temperature correlations, progression, and a heatmap. Tap any data point for details. Filter by time period.

Events

Events on your feed with one-tap RSVP. Weekly look-ahead strip. Cleaner cards with better time display and organizer chips.

Speed

Stats load from pre-computed server data. Caching for near-instant repeat visits. Noticeably snappier overall.

Danish

Follows your phone's language. Labels, achievements, dates, plurals — everything in Danish.

Haptic feedback

A little buzz on fist-bumps, logging swims, and unlocking achievements. Makes everything feel more responsive.

Design & everything else

Inbox readability upgrade. Consistent typography across events and feed. Welcome screen for new members. Help topics throughout. Better keyboard handling. Lots of small fixes on both platforms.
