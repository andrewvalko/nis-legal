What's New

March 2026

Dark mode

The app follows your phone's system setting. If your phone is in dark mode, NIS Tracker is too. Every screen, every chart, every card — all adapted.

Swim photos

You can now attach a photo when logging a swim. It shows up as a big hero image on your feed card — makes scrolling through the feed way more fun. Tap "Add photo" on the log screen, pick an image, done.

Profile photos

Tap your avatar on your profile to upload a real photo. It replaces the colored circle everywhere — feed cards, comments, leaderboards, the works. Or keep the initials if you prefer.

Achievements on your profile

Your earned achievement badges now show on your profile in a grid. Other members can see them too when they visit your profile. There's also a progress bar showing which achievement you're closest to unlocking next.

Haptic feedback

The app now gives you a little buzz when you fist-bump someone's swim, log a swim, or unlock an achievement. Small thing, but it makes everything feel more responsive.

New stats tab

Four new charts: swim trends over time, temperature vs. distance correlations, distance progression, and a heatmap showing when you swim most. Tap any data point for details. Filter by time period with the chips at the top.

Events improvements

Upcoming events now show on your feed with one-tap RSVP. The events tab has a weekly look-ahead strip so you can see what's coming at a glance. Event cards got a visual refresh — cleaner layout, better time display, organizer shown as a chip.

Speed

Stats load from pre-computed data on the server now instead of crunching every swim on your phone. There's caching underneath so repeat visits are near-instant. The whole app should feel noticeably snappier.

Danish language support

The app follows your phone's language setting. Set your phone to Danish and everything — labels, achievements, dates, plurals — shows in Danish.

Design polish

The Inbox got a readability upgrade — larger text, bigger avatars, more breathing room between notifications. Events and the swim feed now use consistent typography and sizing. Time formatting follows your language (English shows "9 am", Danish shows "09:00"). Lots of small visual tweaks across both platforms.

Everything else

Welcome screen for new members. Help topics throughout the app. Privacy controls for private swims. Improved event creation. Better keyboard handling. Lots of small fixes across both iOS and Android.
